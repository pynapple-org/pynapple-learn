Instantiating a Tsd object#

At the heart of pynapple are time series object. The simplest one is the Tsd for Time Series Data. The Tsd object represent one-dimensional time series. It needs two arguments :

  • t : the timestamps

  • d : the data

  • Other options

Question : Can you instantiate a Tsd object called tsd. Note : every pynapple objects are at the root of the namespace.
